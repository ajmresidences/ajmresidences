Your stay at The Courtyards...

Experience the breezy, cold air with the scent of pine trees at the Goshenland Courtyards Condominiums in Baguio City, Philippines. AJM Residences provides the most affordable, relaxing, and family-friendly 2-story loft to make your stay in Baguio feel like a breeze. Located 20 minutes' walk from Baguio City center, it is about 2 to 3 miles away from popular destinations like Mines View Park, Brunham Park, and Lourdes Grotto.

The AJM Residences unit can accomodate up to 7 people and will include:

• Living Room (with sofa bed)
• 1 Bedroom
• 1 Bathroom
• Kitchen (fully furnished with a refrigerator, microwave, electric stove top, electric kettle, oven toaster, and kitchen utensiles)

Other accomodations:

• 24 Hour Security
• Free Wi-fi
• TV Plus
• Hot Water
• Common Parking
• Access to Jeepneys and Taxis

Any other questions? Contact us!

Get to know us...

The Tandoc Family
Owners

Alejandro and Margieta Tandoc are the owners of AJM Residences. Alejandro is a pianist and works as a phlebotomist for American Red Cross. Margieta is a singer, an avid music lover, and also a recent Masters in Nursing Education graduate. Their daughter, Mae,  graduated in Pharmaceutical Business and works as an actress.  Based in the US, Baguio City holds a special place in their hearts as one of top destinations to experience in the Philippines.
